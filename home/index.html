Who are we?

The Pharmaceutical Chemistry Student Union (PCSU) is a student-run organization dedicated to supporting undergraduate students registered in the specialist program. First established by students in 2008, PCSU is continuously evolving to provide resources and enhance students’ university experience, in the forms of class help sessions, academic and career-path seminars, and athletic and social events. Additionally, the PCSU acts as a liaison for open communication between students and faculty. Any student registered in the Pharmaceutical Chemistry program is automatically a member of PCSU, while the team of executive members who lead PCSU are elected annually by the student body.
